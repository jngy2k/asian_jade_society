About the DSNY Jade Society

The DSNY Jade Society combines time-honored traditions with cherished family values to uphold standards of integrity, honor, and courtesy in the community, fostering a profound sense of friendly association and brotherhood among members.

Our mission drives our organization to promote cultural heritage and community service within the Department of Sanitation, creating lasting bonds and positive impact in our neighborhoods.

We are committed to supporting our members, their families, and the broader community through various cultural events, charitable initiatives, and fellowship activities throughout the year.
